Clint's Career\r\n\When Clint first began working, he wanted to be a storm chaser.\r\n\He decked out his truck, and followed storms with his camera.\r\n\After years of near misses, he got some really great tornado shots.\r\n\His most popular images were of lightning, and he got an amazing view.\r\n\He ended up quitting storm chasing to be a photographer.\r\n\
