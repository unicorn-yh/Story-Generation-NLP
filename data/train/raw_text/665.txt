Birthday Beach Trip\r\n\Jack reached his ninetieth birthday last week.\r\n\Jack had never seen the ocean, but spent ninety years hoping to do so.\r\n\He made many sacrifices for his family and was never able to go.\r\n\For his ninetieth birthday, his family took him to the Florida coast.\r\n\Upon finally seeing the ocean, Jack shed a tear of amazement and joy.\r\n\
