Man Eat Dog\r\n\Fred went to the pound to pick up a puppy.\r\n\The clerk there helped him find one he was content with.\r\n\Fred told the clerk he planned on making a roast with it.\r\n\The clerk looked at Fred with disgust and snatched the puppy back.\r\n\Fred expressed bewilderment, as in his culture dog was a delicacy.\r\n\
