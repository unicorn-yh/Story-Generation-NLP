Love Poem\r\n\When Cathy reached home, she decided to check her mailbox.\r\n\There were the typical bills, along with other junk mail.\r\n\However, she noticed a pink envelope addressed from her boyfriend.\r\n\Upon opening, Cathy saw a card with the most beautiful love poem.\r\n\Cathy was moved to tears by the poem that her boyfriend sent her.\r\n\
