Lying compulsion\r\n\Sophie had a big failing and lied all the time.\r\n\Her mother told her that if she lied again, she'd be punished.\r\n\Sophie didn't want to be punished but couldn't help lying.\r\n\When her mother caught her lying again, she sent Sophie to her room.\r\n\Sophie was sad, but by herself, she found more ways to lie next time.\r\n\
