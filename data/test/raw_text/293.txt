Dishonesty\r\n\Silvia had to give a big speech at work.\r\n\She asked her friend Albert to listen to it and give his opinion.\r\n\Albert listened to the speech but wasn't convinced it was good.\r\n\Despite his own dislike for the speech, he complimented Silvia.\r\n\Silvia made her speech and looked like a fool without knowing why.\r\n\
