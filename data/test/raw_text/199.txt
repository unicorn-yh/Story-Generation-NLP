Turtle Hatching\r\n\The crowd waited at the turtle nest, as the eggs would hatch soon.\r\n\An egg began to crack open, and the crowd got very quiet.\r\n\The crowd cleared a path for the baby turtle to enter the water.\r\n\The turtle entered the water successfully and the crowd cheered.\r\n\They then went back to wait for the next egg to hatch.\r\n\
